About Us

IRON and BLOOD is a website designed to teach the circumstances of German unification including the history, the major players, and the iconic "Iron and Blood" speech delivered by German Chancellor Otto von Bismarck.

Our information is factual and trustworthy as the sources are reputable. However, we are in no way experts on the subject matter contained in this website.

Usage

This site is a purely educational site. Our information is intended to inform the public and aims to be as objective as possible. All the information contained on this site is free to use, whether it be for research, education, citation, etc.

Reference us in your work if you feel generous!
